The Duomo

The Duomo in Milan, which is composed of pink-hued white marble from a specific quarry, is the largest and most intricate Gothic structure in Italy. At the top of the main spire, where the gleaming golden statue of the Madonnina is located. The cathedral is 157 meters long and 108.5 meters high.

The famed gem in the Sintra Hills' crown, the National Palace of Pena, stands out.The surrounding park evokes feelings of mystery and discovery since it works so well with the palace's wonderful aura. Our gaze becomes lost among its attractions and its crevices.

Deichman Library

There are 22 neighbourhood libraries in Oslo that make up Deichman. The oldest and largest public library in Norway is Deichman, which opened its doors in 1785. Deichman Bjrvika, Oslo's new central library, is situated between the Opera and Oslo Central Station.
